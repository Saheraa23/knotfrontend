About the Platform

The Virtual Sticky Note Brainstorming Platform is a secure web-based collaboration application designed to make brainstorming sessions simple, organized, and highly effective. Instead of relying on traditional paper sticky notes, teams can instantly create, stack, and manage digital canvas cards on a shared interactive board.

In this project, a session facilitator maintains administrative authority over the workspace architecture, while contributors are empowered to seamlessly add, edit, precisely reposition, and clear sticky notes in real-time. Built on a powerful Spring Boot architecture and secured using JWT authentication.
