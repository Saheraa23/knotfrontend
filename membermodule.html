👥 Board Member Module

Securely invite teammates, assign roles, and collaborate effortlessly in real time.

About Board Member

A Board Member is a registered App User who has been invited to join a Brainstorming Board. Members work together with the board owner by creating, editing, organizing, and managing sticky notes. Every member is assigned an access level that determines what actions they can perform within the board.

The Board Member module establishes the relationship between users and boards. It ensures secure collaboration by allowing only authorized members to access shared resources. This module supports teamwork, communication, accountability, and efficient idea management.

Core Objectives

  • 👤

    Invite Members

    Allow board owners to invite users and build collaborative teams for brainstorming activities.

  • 🔐

    Access Control

    Assign appropriate permissions such as Viewer, Editor, or Admin to ensure secure collaboration.

  • 🤝

    Team Collaboration

    Enable multiple members to share ideas, manage sticky notes, and work together in real time.

  • 🛡️

    Secure Environment

    Protect brainstorming boards by allowing access only to authorized members with valid permissions.

Responsibilities of Board Members

Board Members play an important role in ensuring effective collaboration within the Virtual Sticky Note Brainstorming Platform. They contribute ideas, organize sticky notes, participate in discussions, and work together to achieve project goals.

📝 Create Sticky Notes

Members can create new sticky notes to share ideas, tasks, and important information with the team.

✏ Edit Existing Notes

Authorized members can update sticky notes to improve content and keep project information current.

💬 Collaborate

Work together with teammates by contributing ideas and organizing notes efficiently.

🔒 Follow Permissions

Members must respect assigned access levels and perform only the operations they are authorized to execute.

Benefits of Board Members

  • Better Teamwork: Board Members enable multiple users to work together on the same brainstorming board simultaneously.
  • Faster Decisions: Ideas are shared instantly, allowing teams to make quick and informed decisions.
  • Increased Productivity: Tasks can be distributed among members, improving efficiency and reducing workload.
  • Secure Collaboration: Permission-based access protects confidential information while enabling effective teamwork.

Importance of Board Members

Board Members are the backbone of collaborative brainstorming. They actively participate in discussions, contribute innovative ideas, manage sticky notes, and ensure projects progress efficiently. By assigning different access levels, the system maintains security while promoting effective teamwork.

This module strengthens communication, improves productivity, and enables multiple users to collaborate on a single brainstorming board in a secure environment. Every contribution made by a Board Member helps transform ideas into successful outcomes.
